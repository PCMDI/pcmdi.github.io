IPCC Standard Output

Much of the output from the coupled ocean-atmosphere GCM simulations that are being performed in support of the IPCC process will be archived at PCMDI and then distributed for analysis to IPCC Working Group 1 under policies developed by IPCC and the WGCM.  The following three documents contain the specifications and requirements for this model output:

Standard output description: the list of experiments, the fields that should be saved, and the periods for which they will be analyzed.

Output requirements: the specifications for writing the standard output.

CMOR Software: a fortran code that facilitates adherence to the output requirements.

Data transfer procedure: procedure for preparing and transferring model output to PCMDI.
