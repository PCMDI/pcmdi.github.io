Access to Referenced Documents

Several of the documents referenced in the AMIP II guidelines are not readily available. Some examples are:

Hess, G. D. and B. J. McAvaney, 1997: Note on computing screen temperatures, humidities and anemometer-height winds in large-scale models. Aust. Met. Mag., 46, 109-115. (pdf file)

Hess, G. D., R. A. Colman and B. J. McAvaney, 1995: On computing screen temperatures, humidities and anemometer-height winds in large-scale models. Aust. Met. Mag., 44, 139-145. (pdf file)

Trenberth, K. E., J. C. Berry and L. E. Buja, 1993: Vertical interpolation and truncation of model-coordinate data. NCAR Technical Note NCAR/TN-396+STR, 54 pp.
 

AMIP participants may request references such as these from the AMIP Project Office (amip@pcmdi.llnl.gov). When making a request, please identify the article(s) to be forwarded, and provide a complete mailing address.
