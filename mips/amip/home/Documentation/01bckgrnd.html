History of Changes

This hypertext document traces its origins to the hardcopy 1994 PCMDI Report No. 18, "A Summary Documentation of the AMIP Models" by Thomas J. Phillips. Substantive changes (listed in reverse chronological order) since the publication of PCMDI Report No. 18 include:

  • Change in "vintage" year of MPI ECHAM4 (T42 L19) model from 1995 to 1996 (May 1998)
  • Clarification of method for determining surface evaporation beta parameter in Land Surface Processes of UKMO HADAM1 (2.5x3.75 L19) 1993 model (May 1998)
  • Addition of WWW URL for the Canadian Climate Centre--now the Canadian Centre for Climate Modeling and Analysis (April 1998)
  • Addition of information on treatment of mean land-surface temperature in the CNRM ARPEGE Cy11 (T42 L30) 1995 model (September 1997)
  • Addition of WWW URL for Japan Meteorological Agency (June, 1997)
  • Change of AMIP representative for the GSFC modeling group (December, 1996)


Release of Hypertext Version 1.3 (November 15, 1996)

  • Update of WWW URL in the LLNL model report (November, 1996)
  • Provision of greater detail on orographic smoothing of selected models in Table 2-5 (November, 1996)
  • Change of UKMO model designation from UKMO UM-Climate1 (2.5x3.75) 1993 to UKMO HadAM1 (2.5x3.75) 1993 (October, 1996)
  • Documentation of model MPI ECHAM4 (T42 L19) 1995 used for AMIP revisit experiment (October, 1996)
  • Update of e-mail addresses of AMIP representatives for MPI (October, 1996)
  • Update of World Wide Web URL in NCAR CCM2 model report (September, 1996)
  • Documentation of model CSU CSU95 (4x5 L17) 1995 used for AMIP revisit experiment (September, 1996)
  • Clarifications of descriptions of atmospheric dynamics, planetary boundary layer, and surface fluxes in report on model CSU CSU91 (4x5 L17) 1991 (September, 1996)
  • Correction of errors in description of threshold precipitation parameters in model LMD LMD5 (3.6x5.6) 1991 (August, 1996)
  • Documentation of companion models BMRC BMRC3.7 (R31 L17) 1995 and BMRC BMRC3.7.1 (R31 L17) 1995 used for AMIP revisit experiments (August, 1996)
  • Correction of BMRC BMRC2.3 (R31 L9) 1990 entry for roughness effects of snow cover in Table 2-13.

  • Documentation of model NTU GCM (T42 L13) 1995 of the National Taiwan University, a new AMIP modeling group (August, 1996)

  • Addition of description of special treatment of roughness lengths and surface fluxes for sea-ice leads in baseline model DERF GFDLSM392.2 (T42 L18) 1993; documentation of model DERF GFDLSM195 (T42 L18) 1995 used for repeated AMIP experiment (August, 1996)

  • Addition of information on time integration scheme and snow cover and correction of force-restore time constant for land surface processes in summary report for model CNRM EMERAUDE (T42 L30) 1992 (June, 1996)
  • Addition of information in report on initialization of model MRI GCM-IIb 4x5L15 (May, 1996)
  • Documentation of model DNM A5407.V2 (4x5 L7) 1995 (May, 1996)

  • Inclusion of "vintage year" in all model designations (April, 1996)

  • NCAR model report: addition of WWW address of summaries of CCM2 experiments (March, 1996)

  • Documentation of Model MRI GCM-IIb 4x5L15 used for AMIP revisit experiment; update of Kitoh et al. (1995) reference (November, 1995)
