Arpe, K., L. Bengtsson and E. Roeckner, 1995: Impacts of observed SST on the atmospheric circulation in the ECHAM simulations. Abstracts of the First International AMIP Scientific Conference, Monterey, California, 104.

Impacts of observed SST on the atmospheric circulation will be shown by investigating 5 extended AMIP simulations with the ECHAM3/T42 model. As far as possible the impacts in the simulations will be verified by observations. The stability of these findings will then be investigated by studying simulations with a more recent version of our model - ECHAM4. We will also address some simulations with different resolution.
