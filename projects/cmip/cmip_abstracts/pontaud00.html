Pontaud, M., J-P Ceron, F. Pluviaud, and CMIP1 Participants, 1998:
Le sous-projet DIVCOP de CMIP: Compilation des re'sultats (The DIVCOP subproject of CMIP:  Compilation of results)
Note No. 1, Ecole Nationale de la Meteorologie, Meteo France.

Abstract


The goal of this report is to broadcast rapidly the results of DIVCOP towards the CMIP participants and eventually to an enlarged community. DIVCOP focuses on the behavior of 17 models over the equatorial Pacific i.e. mean state, seasonal cycle and interannual variability. At this stage, only the row results are presented and no full synthesis is proposed. All contributions to the analysis will be welcome.
