CMIP I Requested List of Data

The following output data have been requested from each participating modeling group in CMIP I.


Atmospheric Fields: 

  • DJF and JJA seasonal means (minimum 30-year averages) 

      • geographical distributions  

      • precipitation
      • sea level pressure
      • wind stress (u and v components)
      • net surface heat flux at the air-sea interface
      • net surface freshwater flux at the air-sea interface
      • flux adjustments, if any
      • ice/snow cover--average distribution of depth

      zonal mean cross sections 

      • temperature
      • u-component wind
      • v-component wind
      • moisture
  • time series of monthly mean "surface air temperature" (as defined for each model, e.g. temperature at 2 meters above the surface, at lowest atmospheric vertical level, etc.)
  • variability, cross sections
      • standard deviation of annual mean zonal mean temperature (minimum 30 years)
      • standard deviation of decadal mean zonal mean temperature

Oceanic Fields: 

  • annual means (minimum 30-year averages)
    • zonal mean cross sections of meridional overturning streamfunction, global and by basin
    • vertically integrated 
      • global horizontal mass transport streamfunction
      • zonal mean heat transport, by basin
  • DJF and JJA seasonal means (minimum 30-year averages) 
    • zonal mean cross sections, by basin
      • temperature
      • salinity
    • geographic distributions
      • sea surface temperature\
      • surface salinity



Last update November 14, 1997.  For further information about CMIP model documentation, contact Tom Phillips (phillips@pcmdi.llnl.gov).  For further information about CMIP model data, contact Curt Covey (covey@pcmdi.llnl.gov).
